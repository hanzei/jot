Jot is a self-hosted note-taking app. Connect it to your own Jot server to keep your notes private and under your control.

Features:
- Text notes and todo lists
- Labels and color-coding for organization
- Pin and archive notes
- Offline support with local SQLite storage
- Sync with your self-hosted Jot server
- Share notes with other users on your server
- Dark, light, and system themes
- Multiple language support
