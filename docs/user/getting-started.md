# Getting Started with Jot

Welcome to Jot, your self-hosted note-taking application! This guide will help you get started with creating and managing your notes.

## First Time Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. **Open Jot** in your web browser (usually at `http://localhost:8080`)
2. **Click "Create Account"** on the login page
3. **Enter your details**:
   - Username (minimum 2 characters)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, with at least one letter and one number)
   - Confirm your password
4. **Click "Create Account"**

> 🎉 **Congratulations!** As the first user, you automatically become an administrator with full access to the system.

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:
- **Header**: Contains the Jot logo, navigation tabs, search bar, and user menu
- **Notes Grid**: Where all your notes are displayed
- **New Note Button**: Blue button to create your first note

## Creating Your First Note

### Text Notes

1. **Click the "New Note" button**
2. **Select "Text"** as the note type
3. **Add a title** (optional but recommended)
4. **Write your content** in the text area
5. **Choose a color** (optional) by clicking the color circles
6. **Close the editor** (changes are saved automatically)

### Todo Lists

1. **Click the "New Note" button**
2. **Select "Todo List"** as the note type
3. **Add a title** for your list
4. **Add items** by typing in the text fields
5. **Click "+ Add item"** to add more items
6. **Close the editor** (changes are saved automatically)

## Managing Your Notes

### Viewing Notes

- **Notes Grid**: All your notes are displayed in a grid layout
- **Click any note** to open it for editing
- **Recent notes appear first**, with pinned notes at the top

### Editing Notes

1. **Click on any note** to open the edit modal
2. **Make your changes** to title, content, or items
3. **Change colors** using the color picker
4. **Close the editor** to keep your changes

### Organizing Notes

#### Pinning Notes
- **Click the three dots (⋮)** on any note
- **Select "Pin"** to keep important notes at the top
- **Pinned notes** show a small blue dot indicator

#### Archiving Notes
- **Click the three dots (⋮)** on any note
- **Select "Archive"** to hide notes from your main view
- **View archived notes** by clicking the "Archive" tab in the header

#### Deleting Notes
- **Click the three dots (⋮)** on any note
- **Select "Delete"** and confirm
- **Deleted notes move to Bin** where you can restore or permanently delete them

## Searching and Filtering

### Search Notes
- **Use the search bar** in the header
- **Type keywords** to find notes by title or content
- **Search works in real-time** as you type

### Filter by Archive Status
- **"Notes" tab**: Shows active notes (default view)
- **"Archive" tab**: Shows archived notes only

## Note Features

### Colors
Choose from 6 colors to organize your notes:
- **White**: Default color
- **Yellow**: Great for reminders
- **Green**: Perfect for completed tasks
- **Blue**: Good for information
- **Red**: Ideal for urgent items
- **Purple**: Nice for creative notes

### Todo List Features
- **Check/uncheck items** by clicking the checkboxes
- **Completed items** appear with strikethrough text
- **Reorder items** by editing the note and changing positions
- **Add/remove items** anytime by editing the note

## Tips for Better Note-Taking

### Organization Tips
1. **Use descriptive titles** to find notes quickly
2. **Pin important notes** to keep them visible
3. **Use colors consistently** (e.g., red for urgent, green for completed)
4. **Archive old notes** to keep your workspace clean
5. **Use search regularly** to find specific information

### Productivity Tips
1. **Create daily/weekly todo lists** to stay organized
2. **Break large tasks** into smaller todo items
3. **Use different colors** for different projects or categories
4. **Regular review** your notes and archive what's no longer needed
5. **Keep notes concise** for better readability

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't See My Notes
- Make sure you're on the correct tab (Notes vs Archive)
- Clear your search if you have text in the search bar
- Try refreshing the page

#### Note Won't Save
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you have content in title, text, or todo items
- Try refreshing the page and logging in again

#### Forgot My Password
- Contact your system administrator to reset your password
- As there's no email recovery in self-hosted setups

### Getting Help

If you need additional help:
1. **Check the troubleshooting section** in the main README
2. **Contact your system administrator** if you're not the admin
3. **Check the admin documentation** if you're the administrator
